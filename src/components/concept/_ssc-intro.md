Semgrep Supply Chain is a software composition analysis (SCA) tool that discovers and detects security vulnerabilities in your codebase introduced by open source dependencies. It can also:

- Assist with the triage and remediation of security issues introduced by open source dependencies
- Prevent future issues by preventing the introduction and use of insecure packages through policy definitions
- Detect malicious dependencies and support searches for in-depth information on the packages your project uses to support incident response actions
- Support the enforcement of your business' [open source package licensing requirements](/semgrep-supply-chain/license-compliance)
- Generate reports on the issues present in your projects and software bills of materials (SBOM) that provide a complete inventory of your open source components
