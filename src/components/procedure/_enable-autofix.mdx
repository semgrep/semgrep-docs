To enable autofix for all projects in your Semgrep AppSec Platform organization, follow these steps:

1. In Semgrep AppSec Platform, go to **[Settings > General > Code](https://semgrep.dev/orgs/-/settings/general/code)**.
2. Click the **Autofix** <i class="fa-solid fa-toggle-large-on"></i> toggle to enable this feature.
