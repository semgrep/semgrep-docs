To remove a demo project, follow these steps:

1. In Semgrep App, go to the **[Projects](https://semgrep.dev/orgs/-/projects)** page.
1. Click the <i class="fa-solid fa-gear"></i> **gear** icon of the **Demo project - OWASP Juice Shop**.
1. Click the <i class="fa-regular fa-ellipsis"></i> ellipsis icon, and then click <i class="fa-sharp fa-solid fa-xmark"></i> **Delete project**.
    ![Screenshot of Semgrep App Projects page with the **Delete project** button.](/img/semgrep-app-delete-demo-project.png "Screenshot of Semgrep App Projects page with the **Delete project** button.")
