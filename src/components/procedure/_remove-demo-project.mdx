To remove a demo project, follow these steps:

1. In Semgrep AppSec Platform, go to the **[Projects](https://semgrep.dev/orgs/-/projects)** page.
1. Click the empty checkbox in the **Demo project - OWASP Juice Shop** row, and then click **Delete (1)**.
   ![Screenshot of Semgrep AppSec Platform Projects page when deleting a project.](/img/semgrep-app-delete-demo-project.png "Screenshot of Semgrep AppSec Platform Projects page when deleting a project.")
1. Click **Confirm Delete**.

:::note
You can always add the demo project again if the Semgrep organization you are using does not have any other projects.
:::
